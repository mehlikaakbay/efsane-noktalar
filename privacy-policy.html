Last Updated: January 13, 2026

Efsane ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Efsane Noktalar mobile application (the "App").

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use our App. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

1. Information We Do Not Collect or Store

We do not collect or store any personal data ourselves. Some data (such as location coordinates) may be processed by third-party services strictly to provide app functionality.

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No names, email addresses, or phone numbers collected
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry tools used
  • No advertising networks or data brokers involved
  • No user behavior profiling or monitoring

2. Location Information

Efsane Noktalar uses your device's location services only while the App is actively in use to:

  • Display nearby döner restaurant locations
  • Calculate distances between your location and restaurants
  • Center the map view on your current position

Important details about location usage:

  • Location data is processed locally on your device only
  • Location data is never transmitted to our servers
  • Location data is not stored by the App or by us
  • Location data is processed on your device and used only to display maps and nearby locations through Google Maps SDK. We do not collect or store this data.
  • Location is not used for tracking, profiling, or advertising

Your control:

You can disable location access at any time through your device settings. The App will continue to function, but you will need to manually search for locations or browse the map.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party service to provide core functionality:

Google Maps SDK

  • Purpose: Display interactive maps and restaurant location markers
  • Data processed: Location coordinates (to render the map and show your position)
  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Maps may process location data according to their own privacy practices. We do not receive, collect, or store any data from Google Maps.

4. External Links and System Actions

The App may trigger the following system-level actions initiated by you:

  • Navigation: Opens your device's default map application (e.g., Google Maps, Apple Maps) with directions
  • Phone calls: Opens your device's phone dialer to call a restaurant

These actions are handled entirely by your device's operating system or installed third-party apps. We have no access to or control over these interactions.

5. Children's Privacy

Efsane Noktalar is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us immediately so we can address the situation.

6. Data Security

Because we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, there is no personal data at risk of exposure, breach, or misuse through our App.

All location processing occurs locally on your device.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain full control over location permissions through your device settings.
